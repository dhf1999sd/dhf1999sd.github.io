Biography

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree at Lanzhou University, under the supervision of Prof. Fengyuan Ren, with additional mentorship from Assoc. Prof. Tong Zhang. From Sep. 2021 to Jun. 2024, I received my Master's degree from Lanzhou University. From Sep. 2017 to Jun. 2021, I received my Bachelor's degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University. My work explores the intersection of time-sensitive networking, programmable network hardware, and real-time embedded systems. Here is my CV.
