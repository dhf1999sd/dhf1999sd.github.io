Wenxue Wu (武文学)

Short Bio

  • I am currently an associate research professor at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. From Jul. 2023 to Aug. 2023, I was lucky to visit Prof. Jiangchuan Liu's group at Simon Fraser University as a visiting scholar. From Jan. 2020 to Jun. 2023, I was a postdoc at Beijing Institute of Technology working with Prof. Hua Huang. In Jan. 2020, I received my Ph.D. in computer science and technology from Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Jihong Zhu. Before that, I received my B.S. from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2014.
  • My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, edge computing and robotics.
